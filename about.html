About DDR

Dolma Dog Rescue is a non-profit dedicated to helping stray dogs in need of medical treatment and care in Bylakuppe, South India. The rescue house was started by Tsering Dolma, who wanted to take an initiative to make a change for the many stray dogs in desperate need of help. Dolma is based in the US and has been funding the rescue house with her savings since 2018. Her Brother Kalsang works as the administrator and looks after DDR's daily operations. DDR is now a safe haven for over more than 30 dogs and it has appointed three helpers and a veterinarian. Learn more about DDR in the following video.
