Make a Difference for a Furry Friend Today

Dolma Dog Rescue relies heavily on donations for it's operation. We are expanding and new stray dogs are admitted at the rescue house frequently. That's why we need your help! Please consider making a one time or a monthly donation. Monthly donation can be canceled anytime. We also accept Venmo donations @dolma-dog-rescue. Please note that DDR only accept donations via our website, Venmo, Zelle, or in person at the rescue center. Thank you so much for being kind and caring for our animals.
