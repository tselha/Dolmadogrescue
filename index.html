Donate

Dolma Dog Rescue is able to operate solely from donations made by individuals who care for our dogs. Your donation will help the center with necessary resources such as grooming supplies, medicines, food, transportation, etc. Please donate if you can! Click here to donate.

Sponsor

You can sponsor a dog and it is a great way to help the dogs who cannot be put back out on the street due to medical issues. Many of the dogs at the rescue house aren't able to get adopted due to underlying medical conditions. If you are interested in sponsoring a dog please contact us.

Volunteer

DDR is open to volunteers! Volunteering duties include walking our dogs, grooming, feeding, help with cleaning, and most importantly fundraising! Which is perfect for those who can help virtually. Contact us if you'd like to volunteer for us.

Adopt

DDR welcome local adoptions. Our dogs are fully vaccinated and all they need is a loving home. Please contact us if you are interested in adopting DDR pets.

Share

Please share Dolma Dog Rescue on your social media platforms. We believe that simply sharing the work we do can lead to future adoptions and sponsorships for our dogs. Visit our Instagram and Facebook
